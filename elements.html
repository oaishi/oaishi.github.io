Achievements

Learn It Girl! Featured Project

This is a global mentorship program with the aim to help students learn and build something new within a span of three months. I was supervised by Himanshi Jain from Delhi Technological University to build a mobile application. Among the 126 projects, PocketAid got featured along with 11 other projects as the best ones on their official website .

MobilPro 2018, Smart Health, Participant

I applied for Smart Health project showcase section, MobilPro 2018. After an online screening, I got selected for the global round and pitched my application over a skype session.

Banglalink SDG Hackathon 2018 , 1st Runner's Up

Banglalink, in partnership with Preneur Lab – Social Good Company and Google Developer Group Dhaka (GDG Dhaka) organized SDG Hackathon-Code for a Cause for the coders’ community and Problem-solvers of Bangladesh. The theme of this 24-hour long hackathon is based on Sustainable Development Goals and its 5 selected thematic areas. Our team appeared as the 1st Runner's Up in the final evaluation.

Buet CSE Fest Hackathon 2019 , Champion - Cloud Computing

We were asked to build a chatbot which could detect mood from image and detect songs depending on the user's mood. We hosted our chatbot on Telegram - which could crawl meme, jokes and lyrics as well.

Hack_A_Day 2019 , Champion - Mental Health Problem in Bangladesh

We were asked to build a feasible solution in respect of Bangladesh, to tackle mental health problems. We designed an android chatbot application which monitors the user's activities and uses Machine Learning to effectively detect depression and suggest thereby.

Banglalink Ennovators - Finalist

We were assigned to build a feasible solution for sustainability. We designed an android application that empowers women by allowing them sewing and earning while staying indoor.

Hello

I am Faria Huq


CV | Github | Google Scholar | Twitter | LinkedIn | Medium | Youtube


My interest is in improving how machines can interpret and reason from the 3D data more efficiently. To enable this, I focus on using Computer Graphics, Geometric Processing and Deep Learning- especially in the context of Virtual Reality applications and Interactive System design.

Please visit my Research and Projects Pages to see the detailed list of my completed and ongoing works.

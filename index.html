PhD Student, Human-Computer Interaction Institute

Human-Agent Collaboration and Collective Reasoning with AI

I study human-agent collaboration, personalization in agentic AI systems, and AI support for collective reasoning. My current work investigates how AI can better collaborate with people on the web and how AI mediation reshapes group decision-making.

CV Google Scholar GitHub LinkedIn X / Twitter

Research Themes

Human-Agent Collaboration

I design interactive agent systems that let people and AI coordinate effectively on complex web tasks.

Representative work: CowPilot, PlowPilot

Personalized Agentic AI

I study how agents can model user preferences, interaction styles, and partial human input to become better collaborators.

Representative work: NoTeeline, CowPilot, PlowPilot

AI for Collective Reasoning

I investigate how AI mediation reshapes group structure, information sharing, and decision quality in opinion-diverse teams.

Representative work: AI-Mediated Communication Reshapes Social Structure in Opinion-Diverse Groups

Interactive Systems with Language Models

I build AI interfaces that support real-world tasks such as note-taking, information retrieval, and adaptive assistance.

Representative work: NoTeeline, What’s Important Here?, DreamStruct

Featured Publications

AI-Mediated Communication Reshapes Social Structure in Opinion-Diverse Groups
F Huq, E Claggett, H Shirado
Studies how AI mediation changes network structure and collaboration dynamics in diverse groups.
Preprint Under Review, 2026.

Modeling Distinct Human Interaction in Web Agents
F Huq*, ZZ Wang*, Z Guo, VA Arangarajan, T Ou, FF Xu, S Zhou, G Neubig, JP Bigham
Examines how human web behavior differs from agent behavior and why those differences matter for agent design.
Preprint Under Review, 2026.

CowPilot: A Framework for Autonomous and Human-Agent Collaborative Web Navigation
F Huq, ZZ Wang, FF Xu, T Ou, S Zhou, JP Bigham*, G Neubig*
Introduces a framework for studying how people and web agents share control during real task execution.
Project Page NAACL Demo Track, 2025.

NoTeeline: Supporting Real-Time Notetaking from Keypoints with Large Language Models
F Huq*, A Samee*, DC Lin, XA Tang, JP Bigham
Builds a note-taking assistant that expands user keypoints into personalized notes in real time.
Preprint IUI 2025.
